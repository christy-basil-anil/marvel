Marvel’s Heroes and Villains

Marvel’s universe is defined by its larger-than-life heroes and complex villains. Whether it's the selfless courage of Captain America, the unwavering strength of Thor, or the brilliant mind of Iron Man, each hero brings something unique to the Marvel Universe. But it’s not just about the heroes—Marvel's villains, like the cunning Loki and the universe-threatening Thanos, create challenges and conflicts that elevate every story. The battles between heroes and villains drive the heart of the Marvel Universe, ensuring that the stakes are always high.
